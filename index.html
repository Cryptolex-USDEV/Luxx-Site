About Luxx Studios

Luxx Studios was founded in early 2022 with the desire and experience to bring luxury items to the metaverse. Our talented staff shares more than 40 years of 3D modeling, game and world design, VR/AR implementation, Web3, NFT, and coding experience. We have the knowledge to not only make it but make it work across all metaverse platforms.

The dedicated staff at Luxx Studios aims to help break down the web3 and metaverse walls with cross compatibility in mind. All Luxx products are made with low face counts, fully wrapped 2K/4K texture and UV maps as well as a wide variety of options ranging from cloths to entire worlds. With Luxx Studios only your imagination is the limit.

A Luxxtopian is born the moment someone purchases a Luxx Studios membership or product.
